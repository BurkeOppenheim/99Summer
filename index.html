99 Summer Street Boston is a 271,000 square-foot first class office building in the heart of the Financial District. The property is undergoing a major makeover, slated for completion in early 2018. The project will feature a complete renovation of the building’s lobby and mezzanine level highlighted by several updates and additions.

99 Summer Street is conveniently accessible to all MBTA public transportation systems. It is one block from South Station, and within immediate walking distance to Downtown Crossing, Chinatown, and Park Street Stations. The iconic building provides immediate access to the innovation clusters of Cambridge and the Seaport District. It is part of the surging Business Improvement District boasting many new residential, retail, and hotel developments.

99 Summer is a great opportunity for companies looking for quality, high profile office space in the heart of Downtown.
Amenities include:
Excellent natural light
12’6” ceiling height, slab to slab
Unparalleled views of the city and water
Newly modernized passenger elevators
Numerous retail amenities in the building and immediate neighborhood
Over 120 parking spaces on-site
Flexible floor plates to accommodate users of varying sizes
Easily accessible to all commuter types
24/7 hour security and a card access control system
Rock solid tenant base
